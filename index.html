What is F1Tenth racing?

F1Tenth is a one-tenth scale autonomous racing competition. Using lidar scanners and the NVIDIA Jetson platform, we develop software that enables the car to navigate a race track as quickly as possible -- completing as many laps as possible before crashing into a wall or another car.
